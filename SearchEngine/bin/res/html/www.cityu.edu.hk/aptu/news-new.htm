Conference

December 4, 2017

The 2017 Workshop on Applications of Atom Probe Tomography (2017三維原子探針應用研討會) co-organized by Prof. C.T. Liu and Dr. Z.B. Jiao was held on December 4-5, 2017 at CityU, Hong Kong. This workshop attacted more than 80 participants from various universities/institutes, such as Institue of Metals Research, Chongqing Univeristy, Central South Univeristy, Nanjing U of Sci & Tech, Shanghai U, HKU, CityU, PolyU, and CAMECA. Thank all participants for making the wordshop a success!

May 2017 • No. 54, CityU Today - Cover Story

Probing the future of advanced materials 探索未來的先進材料

 

Nature • Cover: Powerful Alloy

A research team led by Professor Jian Lu, Director of CASM has achieved a ground-breaking advancement in materials research by successfully developing the first-ever supra-nano magnesium alloy. The innovation has been published in top academic journal Nature.

 

Nature Materials • Cover: Materials Take to the Sky

A joint work by Prof. CT Liu and NJUST researchers has achieved a breakthrough in the development of next-generation TiAl alloys with light weight and superior creep resistance, which could provide expanded opportunities for aeronautics and aerospace applications. The innovation has been published in Nature Materials.

Our joint paper with Dr. Yang Lu on "High-Entropy Alloy (HEA)-Coated Nanolattice Structures and Their Mechanical Properties" was accepted for publication in Advanced Engineering Materials.

Aug 7, 2017
Our paper on "Atom-probe study of Cu and NiAl nanoscale precipitation and interfacial segregation in a nanoparticle-strengthened steel" was accepted for publication in Materials Research Letters.

Our paper on "Ultrahigh-strength steels strengthened by nanoparticles" was accepted for publication in Science Bulletin. It highlighted the recent Nature paper on "Ultrastrong steel via minimal lattice misfit and high-density nanoprecipitation" by Prof. Zhaoping Lu's group at USTB. 

Jul 12, 2017
Our joint paper with Prof. J.J. Kai on "Heterogeneous precipitation behavior and stacking-fault-mediated deformation in a CoCrNi-based medium-entropy alloy" was accepted for publication in Acta Materialia.
