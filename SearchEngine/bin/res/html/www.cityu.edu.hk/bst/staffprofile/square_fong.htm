Research Interests

  • Renewable cooling and heating
  • Sustainable trigeneration
  • Zero carbon building design
  • Indoor environmental quality
  • Evolutionary computation
  • Heuristic optimization

Dr. Square K. F. Fong has the academic interest in renewable cooling and heating, sustainable trigeneration and evolutionary computation. He has over a hundred publications in the spectrum of international journal papers, conference papers, book chapters and consultancy reports. Through exercising both the academic and professional knowledge, Dr. Fong aims at developing the zero carbon building technologies for sustainable urbanization.
