Workshop on Preventing Sexual Harassment in the Workplace

預防工作場所的性騷擾工作坊

 

Target Participants : All Staff

Class Size : 24 pax

Date  : 26 March 2015 (Thursday)

Time : 2:30 p.m. - 6:00 p.m. (3.5 hours)

Venue : AC3 6-207

Language of Instruction : Cantonese

Instructor : Professional Trainer from a Training Consultancy Firm

 

Objectives

Sexual harassment is harmful, not only to the perceived victim and harasser, but also to the entire workplace. What separates innocent friendly vs. engaging in an unwelcome behaviour or conduct of a sexual nature is a fine line, and often depends on cultural specific, situational and contextual situations.

 

In this highly practical, engaging and interactive workshop, we will explore ways to recognize, prevent and deal with sexual harassment in the workplace. It will give participants practical insights into the subject from a real life perspective.

 

Content

 What constitutes sexual harassment?

 What is “acceptable behaviour” in line with City University’s Code of Conduct?

 What is “acceptable behaviour” in line with guidelines set forth by the Equal Opportunities Commission?

 Cultural difference and how that affects personally acceptable behaviour boundaries

 Dealing with emotions in face of sexual harassment

 Preventative measures - Ways to foster mutual understanding and respect

 Remedial measures - Ways to resolve differences and conflicts

 Legal implications of sexual harassment

Remarks

The course is subject to cancellation due to under-subscription.
