The Alumni Relations Office helps engage our alumni living in 50 countries and regions with their alma mater by regular communications and joint efforts with alumni ambassadors there. The ambassadors have been contributing greatly by supporting reunions, strengthening connection with alumni fellows, providing mentorship services for exchange students etc. To get involved in our off-shore chapters, please contact our Alumni Ambassadors shown below. If you want to share information about off-shore chapters, please contact our Associate Director Mr Xavier Wong (xavier.wong@cityu.edu.hk) or Alumni Relations Manager, Ms Pheony Tsang (aaphet@cityu.edu.hk).
