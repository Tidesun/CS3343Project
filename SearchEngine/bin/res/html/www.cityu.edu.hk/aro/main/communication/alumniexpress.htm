AlumExpress (Alumni E-News)

Check out the bi-weekly “AlumExpress” to get quick update on University development, events, services, benefits, and more. The e-news is emailed to alumni registered on the University database. If you are a CityU alumnus but fail to receive it, please click HERE to update your email address.

If you are interested in submitting upcoming event information, please contact Ms Joyce Siu, Alumni Relations Manager (joyce.siu@cityu.edu.hk). The Alumni Relations Office reserves the right to edit and publish the information.

For more updates and alumni interviews, please visit the homepage and facebook of the Alumni Relations Office.
