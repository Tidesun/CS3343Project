Conference Location: City University of Hong Kong

CityU is a rapidly growing institution with a burgeoning local and global reputation for professional education and research development. Centrally located at the heart of Asia’s World City, CityU is in Kowloon Tong, next to a transfer station of two major rail routes and a major shopping mall. CityU is strongly committed to creating an internationalised space for professional education, pursuing a research agenda designed to benefit society, establishing a culture of innovation and discovery, and nurturing the talents of our students. To know more about CityU, please visit www.cityu.edu.hk.

Recent achievements of CityU in global rankings highlight the strength of our research, internationalisation, reputation, faculty, students and programmes. In the QS survey 2018, CityU ranks 49th in Worldwide Recognition Ranking and 4th among Top 50 Universities under Age 50. CityU comes first in Engineering/Technology/Computer Sciences in Hong Kong in ARWU survey 2016 and is ranked 2nd in Business School in Asia-Pacific region in UT Dallas survey 2017.
