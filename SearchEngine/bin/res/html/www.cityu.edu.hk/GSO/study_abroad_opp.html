Other Study Abroad Opportunities

CityU encourages students to enhance their global perspectives and multicultural sensitivity through international learning opportunities. Apart from the regular exchange, internship and community service programmes organised by the University, students are also encouraged to join other learning activities outside Hong Kong on full fee-paying basis. Participation in these programmes is self-initiated and voluntary. Students should prepare to manage the programmes and activities independently. Interested students please send your enquiries and applications to the programme organizers directly.

Disclaimer: City University of Hong Kong does not bear any liability, damage and/or claim of any nature whatsoever arising out of, or in any way related to student's participation in any of these programmes and activities.
