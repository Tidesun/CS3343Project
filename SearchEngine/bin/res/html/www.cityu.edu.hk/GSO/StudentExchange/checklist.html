Checklist


    Passport and Visa

    • If you do not have a passport (or a Home Visit Permit for travelling to Mainland China), please apply for one if needed.

    • If you have a passport, please ensure it will be valid for at least six months after the end of your journey.

    • Find out if you need a visa and apply for the necessary documents should you need to.


    Study Plan

    • Confirm your study plan with your host university and home department.


    Transportation

    • Decide how you will travel and purchase the relevant tickets.

    • Familiarize yourself with the customs and layout of the city you wish to travel.

    • Send your arrival schedule to your host university.

    • Plan your route from the airport or train station to your host university.


    Finance

    • Pay or arrange for someone to pay your CityU tuition fee while you are away.

    • Take a credit card with you.


    Luggage

    • Your passport, with the valid visa attached, in your hand-carried luggage.

    • Your air or train ticket.

    • The admission letter issued by your host university.

    • A copy of your insurance policy.

    • Any medication prescribed by your doctor (preferably with a letter from the doctor).

    • Some cash for your initial expenses.

    • Necessary clothing for the climate you will be travelling to.

    • A list of telephone numbers such as contact numbers of your host university, the nearest Chinese Embassy or Consulate General, and your insurance company.


    Others

    • Ensure you are fit and healthy to travel!

    • Make accommodation arrangements.

    • Obtain travel and medical insurance.

    • Leave your insurance policy with your family and bring a copy with you during your exchange.

    • Bring an electrical adaptor should you require one.

    • Check the orientation schedule of your host university.
