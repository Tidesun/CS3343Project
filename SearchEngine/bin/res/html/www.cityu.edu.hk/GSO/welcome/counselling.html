COUNSELLING

Staffed by a team of professional counsellors, the Personal Counselling Service of Student Development Services (SDS) helps students overcome personal and emotional issues. All information is kept strictly confidential. Appointments can be made by phone at 3442 8478 during office hours and by email at sds@cityu.edu.hk. Or you can simply walk in and talk to the on-duty counsellor if you need immediate help.

For more information, please visit the SDS website here.
