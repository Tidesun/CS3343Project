About Us

This website aims to introduce staff and students about the current development and activities of the CityU tea room. We will introduce the Japanese Way of Tea into different aspects such as the background of the tea master, the way of drinking tea, arts, architecture, history and philosophy. In addition, we will hold regular guided tours to the tea room. Students and staff are welcome to register for one. We also provide information about a related CityU General Education course, GE1121.
