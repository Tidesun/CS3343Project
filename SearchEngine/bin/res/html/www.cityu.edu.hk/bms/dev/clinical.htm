Clinical Attachment

It is a compulsory course to all BSc in Biomedical Sciences students. It allows students to get hands-on experience in real-life laboratory setting, and gain a better perspective of laboratory based career.

Students pursue clinical attachment work at local and international hospitals, government agencies and private clinics.
