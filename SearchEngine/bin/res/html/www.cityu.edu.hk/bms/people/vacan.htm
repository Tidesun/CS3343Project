Vacancies

Faculty Positions Available

Chair Professor/Professor/Associate Professor/Assistant Professor
[Ref. A/133/09]

Duties

Teach undergraduate and postgraduate courses, conduct cutting edge research, and develop new research directions and courses.

Requirements

A PhD/MD/DVM or an equivalent degree in the broadly-defined areas of biomedical sciences. The Department currently has active research in molecular, cellular and systems study of neuroscience, cancer, or regenerative biology, and the development of novel therapeutics and nanomedicine. Candidates with strong background in these and other biomedical science-related areas are welcome to apply. Preference will be given to qualified candidates whose activities can contribute to multidisciplinary and collaborative biomedical research.

Candidates for the post of Professor and above should have a distinguished academic record including significant external funding support, demonstrated leadership skills, and a strong commitment to graduate and undergraduate education. Successful candidates are expected to develop internationally competitive research programs; and assume academic and administrative leadership in the Department by assisting in faculty development, promoting collaboration in cross-disciplinary teaching and research programs within the University, and facilitating the translation of basic biomedical research to practices of health services and healthcare-related industries.

Closing Date

Applications will be considered until the positions are filled.

Senior Research Associate / Research Associate
(Appointment for 12 months)
[Ref. C/R/157/09]

Duties

Responsible for the operation of imaging and cellular biology instrumentation for research projects and related activities.

Requirements

A good honours degree in Science or related disciplines with 2 years’ laboratory experience. Good command of written and spoken English and Chinese, and strong work ethics with self-motivation are required. Familiarity with imaging and cellular biology instrumentation is preferred. Candidates with a Master’s degree and 5 years’ laboratory experience may be considered for appointment as Senior Research Associate. (Those who have responded to the previous advertisement in September 2018 need not re-apply.)

Closing Date

2 November 2018

More information and application

Postdoctoral Fellow / Senior Research Assistant
(Appointment for 6 months with possibility of extension)
[Ref. C/R/163/09]

Project

Inhibitory Gating of Neural Circuits for Dynamic Representation of Distinct Odor Features

Duties

Carry out research activities for the Project.

Requirements

A Master’s degree in Engineering/Biology or related disciplines with at least 1 year’s relevant experience. Interest in Neuroscience with strong command of written and spoken English; strong organizational, interpersonal skills and work ethics with self-motivation are required. Experience in MATLAB, brain electrophysiology (in vivo or in vitro), optogenetics, stereotaxic surgery and injection in mice are preferred. Candidates with a PhD in Engineering/Neuroscience/Biology and no more than 3 years’ postdoctoral experience may be considered for appointment as Postdoctoral Fellow.

Closing Date

19 October 2018

Senior Research Assistant / Research Assistant
(Appointment for 3 months with possibility of extension)
[Ref. C/R/156/09]

Project

Establishing a Research Platform to Study Neuropsychiatric Lupus in Human and Murine Model

Duties
